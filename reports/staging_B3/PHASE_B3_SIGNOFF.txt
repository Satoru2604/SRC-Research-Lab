Extracting the Bundle:

  sha256sum -c release/B3_FINAL_BUNDLE.tar.tar.xz.sha256

Running Audit Verification:

  python3 scripts/verify_audit_consistency.py \
    --manifest release/B3_FINAL_MANIFEST.json \
    --output reports/B3_AUDIT_VALIDATION.json \
    --full

Rebuilding the Bundle:

  python3 scripts/rebuild_release_bundle.py \
    --output release/B3_FINAL_BUNDLE_rebuilt.tar.xz \
    --manifest release/B3_FINAL_MANIFEST_rebuilt.json

Inspecting Manifest:

  jq '.release, .phases' release/B3_FINAL_MANIFEST.json
  jq '.artifacts[] | {path, phase, size_bytes}' release/B3_FINAL_MANIFEST.json

Extracting Legacy Bundles:

  tar -xJf release/legacy/PHASE_B1_SIGNOFF_BUNDLE.tar.xz
